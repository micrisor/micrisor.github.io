I am a physicist interested in interdisciplinary research, and passionate about sharing and communicating the excitement of science and the natural world. I am currently a Research Fellow at Trinity College, University of Cambridge and a Borysiewicz Biomedical Fellow at the Cancer Research UK Cambridge Institute, where I work on predictive models of cancer treatment based on imaging data. I previously worked at Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center in New York.

I also lead the programme on Artificial Intelligence innovation in healthcare at the Center for the Governance of Change at IE University in Madrid. I was previously co-chair of the Science and Education Policy Association in New York.
